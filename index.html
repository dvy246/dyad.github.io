About Me

With a background in B.Com Honors, I made a strategic career switch to the exciting world of AI and Machine Learning. This transition was fueled by my passion for solving real-world problems using data-driven approaches and my fascination with how AI can transform businesses.

During my commerce studies, I discovered that the most interesting aspects were always related to data analysis and predictive modeling. I realized that my analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities were perfectly suited for the data science field, which led me to pursue this career change.

I believe that data has the power to transform businesses and improve lives. My unique combination of business knowledge and technical skills allows me to bridge the gap between data insights and business strategy, bringing a valuable perspective to AI projects.

I'm constantly learning and expanding my knowledge in this rapidly evolving field, with a particular interest in developing AI solutions that are both innovative and practical.
